# Switch

A toggle switch styled on top of a native checkbox input. The checkbox carries `role="switch"` so assistive tech announces it as a toggle rather than a two-state checkbox.

<Demo>
  <div class="switch">
    <input class="switch__input" type="checkbox" role="switch" id="demo-switch-1" />
    <label class="switch__toggle" for="demo-switch-1"></label>
  </div>
</Demo>
