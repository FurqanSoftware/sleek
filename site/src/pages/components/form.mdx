# Form

Sleek styles native form controls with a consistent look. Use `.form__group` to wrap a field+label+hint, `.form__label` for labels, and `.form__field` for inputs.

## Basic

<Demo>
  <div class="form__group">
    <label class="form__label" for="demo-email">Email</label>
    <input class="form__field" id="demo-email" type="email" placeholder="name@example.com" />
  </div>
</Demo>

## Required label

<Demo>
  <div class="form__group">
    <label class="form__label -required" for="demo-name">Full name</label>
    <input class="form__field" id="demo-name" type="text" />
  </div>
</Demo>

## With hint

<Demo>
  <div class="form__group">
    <label class="form__label" for="demo-handle">Handle</label>
    <input class="form__field" id="demo-handle" type="text" />
    <div class="form__hint">Between 3 and 20 characters.</div>
  </div>
</Demo>

## Invalid state
