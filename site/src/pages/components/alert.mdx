# Alert

Alerts are notification boxes for surfacing messages to the user. They come in six color variants (default, ebony, blue, orange, green, red) plus a subtle style.

## Variants

<Demo>
  <div class="alert -default">Default — neutral message</div>
  <div class="alert -ebony mt-1">Ebony — dark neutral</div>
  <div class="alert -blue mt-1">Blue — informational</div>
  <div class="alert -green mt-1">Green — success</div>
  <div class="alert -orange mt-1">Orange — warning</div>
  <div class="alert -red mt-1">Red — danger</div>
